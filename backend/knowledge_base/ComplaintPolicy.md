# Complaint Resolution Policy

Customers can raise complaints regarding:

- Damaged products
- Defective products
- Wrong item delivered
- Delivery delays
- Poor customer service

Complaint Process:

1. Contact customer support.
2. Share your Order ID.
3. Provide photos if the product is damaged.
4. Our team reviews the complaint.
5. Resolution is usually provided within 3–5 business days.
