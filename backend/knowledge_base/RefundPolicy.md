# Refund Policy

## Refund Eligibility

Customers may request a refund within **7 days** of receiving their product.

## Conditions

- Product must be unused and in its original packaging.
- Original invoice is required.
- Accessories and manuals must be returned with the product.

## Non-Refundable Items

Refunds are not available for:

- Physical damage caused by the customer
- Missing accessories
- Products returned after 7 days

## Refund Process

1. Contact customer support.
2. Submit your order ID.
3. Our team will inspect the request.
4. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.
